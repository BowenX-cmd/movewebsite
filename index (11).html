MOVE System Architecture

eAxle Technology

  • 360kW peak power per axle, 160kW continuous
  • 13-16 tonne mass rating, water-cooled
  • IP67 ingress protection, electronic differential
  • Hub reduction drive, disc brakes, dual motors

Battery System

  • 352kWh per battery pack, swappable design
  • 1.5 million kWh cycle life over 6 years
  • 656VDC nominal voltage, 4C discharge rate
  • Liquid thermal management, 200km range

Kinetic Kingpin

  • 450kN load sensing capability
  • Measures longitudinal loads to adjust eAxle power
  • Neutralises kingpin load via intelligent feedback
  • Wireless communication with tractor unit

Control System

  • Proprietary software for intelligent power distribution
  • Load-based torque vectoring across multiple axles
  • Regenerative braking optimisation
  • Real-time data logging and telematics

How It Works

1
Kingpin Load Sensing

Kinetic Kingpin measures longitudinal forces and weight distribution to optimise power delivery

2
Intelligent Power Distribution

Control system calculates required torque based on road conditions, gradient, and vehicle speed

3
eAxle Drive System

Electric motors in trailer axles provide additional propulsion, reducing load on tractor engine

4
Regenerative Braking

Kinetic energy during braking is converted back to electricity and stored in batteries

5
Battery Management

Swappable battery packs ensure minimal downtime and extend operational range
